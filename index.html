    How does Gigaflow work?

    How Good is Gigaflow?

    *Higher Cache Hit Rate

    Gigaflow delivers up to 51% higher cache hit rate (avg. 25%) on real-world pipelines

    *Lower Cache Misses

    Gigaflow reduces cache misses by up to 90% and uses 18% lesser cache entries

    *Bigger Rule Space Coverage

    Gigaflow captures up to 450x bigger rule space and thrives in dynamic environments

    * Gigaflow (GF) compared against Megaflow (MF) on real-world pipelines (OFD, PSC, OLS, ANT, OTL) in high/low locality environments

    What is the key insight?

    Pipeline-Aware Locality

    Unlike Megaflow cache which captures traversals as cache entries, Gigaflow partitions them and caches sub-traversals in a multi-table architecture. Optimal selection of sub-traversals allows Gigaflow to capture a power form of locality, called Pipeline-Aware Locality.

    Disjointedness Property

    To capture pipeline-aware locality efficiently, Gigaflow capitalizes on the inherent disjointedness in vSwitch traversals to maximize the cross-product rule space coverage in its multi-table pipeline.
